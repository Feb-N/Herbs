Angelica

Angelica sinensis is a perennial herb belonging to the umbrella family. It tastes sweet, spicy and warm. It belongs to the liver, heart and spleen. It has a long history of naturalization, which has been recorded in China's first pharmaceutical monograph, Shennong Herbal Classic. Most of the traditional Chinese medicine prescriptions are inseparable from Angelica, so there is a saying of "ten prescriptions and nine returns". Angelica has the ability to regulate blood and replenish blood. It has always been regarded as the first medicine in gynecology, known as "the holy medicine in blood"
