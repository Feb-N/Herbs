Prunella vulgaris

Prunella vulgaris is a perennial herbaceous plant. The picking season is mainly summer. Cold nature, bitter taste, bitter, belongs to the liver meridian and gallbladder meridian. Its functions include clearing fire, clearing eyes, loose knots, and swelling; it is a subordinate of heat-clearing medicine. It is born on roadsides, hillsides, fields and grass; it is good for people's spiritual aspects and can calm the mind.
